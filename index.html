Feature of Famous Artworks

Starry Night Over Gogh

Vincent van Gogh's masterpiece captures the night sky over a French village. This iconic painting is celebrated for its bold colors, dramatic impasto, and emotional intensity, representing one of the most recognizable works in modern art.

The Scream Edvard Munch

Edvard Munch's expressionist icon depicts a figure overwhelmed by existential dread against a turbulent sky. This profound exploration of anxiety and isolation has become a powerful symbol of modern emotional and psychological turmoil.
